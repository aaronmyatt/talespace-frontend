About Us

⇒ What is Talespace?

  • TaleSpace is an educational organization focusing on experiential learning through knowledge sharing. It is a space where everybody’s story plays a part in our educational escapade. A story of an individual can be shared as an applicable skill in a community. This learning process is open to all and designed to reach rural and underprivileged communities.

⇒ How it Works?
  • ♦ TaleSpace is a social enterprise which allows a person to volunteer while transferring their knowledge. We will guide on the how to.
  • ♦ A volunteer who joins us is called a TaleMaster.
  • ♦ A TaleMaster signs up to volunteer; is encouraged to help the underprivileged as well.
⇒ Our Mission
  • ♦ To create “spaces” all around the world where anyone can share his or her skills to the local communities.
  • ♦ To enable travelers/to-be-travelers to share their knowledge to the local communities & be rewarded while doing so.
